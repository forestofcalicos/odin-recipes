Flan

This sweet desert is easy to make, and doesn't take much prep time

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of flan
  • 400ml of milk

Instructions

  1. Boil the Milk
  2. once the milk has boiled, take off the heat and add the packet
  3. mix thouroughly for about a minute
  4. pour into a ramekin and refrigerate for 2 hours
